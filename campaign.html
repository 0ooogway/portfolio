Case study Parallel TCG 'Deception' expansion Sponsored · US$20K Sold out on release

The $20,000
Campaign.

A sponsored expansion launch, pitched, closed and delivered end to end.

My team and I built the full creator content package for Parallel TCG's 'Deception' launch. We curated five creator decks and shipped each one with a designed one-pager, a written guide on Medium and a video guide, then capped it all off with a live launch party featuring twelve creators.

01 — overview

the brief.

◆ campaign goal

drive players + educate the community

Drive player acquisition and community education for the 'Deception' expansion launch. Each deck needed to be approachable for new players while showing off the new cards. The idea was to turn a set launch into content people could actually learn from and play.

◆ the approach

one package, five decks

We curated five archetypes with community creators and produced a complete guide package for each one: a designed one-pager, a written walkthrough and a video guide. A launch party broadcast tied it all together on release day.
